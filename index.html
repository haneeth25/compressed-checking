    About Us

    Team - ECOAMIGOS - For a sustainable revolution; a startup based on GO-GREEN supported by AIC-ALEAP WEHUB. Based on the concept of best out of waste and considering the planet's sustainability, we began producing paper from crop residue,such as rice straw and dried wastes of plants. Because crop residue burning is a major concern in India, it could be avoided by converting them into biodegradable paper and cutlery products derived from natural and food-grade resources and this products can be act as the alternatives of single use plastic.
